Lasagna

A delicious lasagna recipe you'll want to make again and again

Pastas, meat sauce and cheese. A match made in heaven yet with the power to make you sell your soul

Ingredients

  • 1 package lagagna noodles
  • 1 pound lean ground beef
  • salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 clove of garlic
  • 1/2 pound shredded mozarella cheerse
  • 1/2 pound shredded Cheddar cheese
  • 1 pint ricotta cheese

Steps

  1. Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil. Add pasta and cook for 8to 10 minutes or until al dente. Drain
  2. Preheate oven to 350degrees F. In a large skillet over medium heat, brown beef and season with saltand pepper. Stir in spaghetty souce and gardic and summer 5 minutes
  3. Combine cheeses.
  4. Layer mixes and pasta.
  5. Bake for 30 minutes.
